The chronology of the ownership, custody, or origin of an object or piece of information. In knowledge engineering, provenance tracks where a fact came from and how it was transformed on its way to the current representation.

Applied to legal documents, provenance answers: was this clause copied verbatim from a template ([[Boilerplate]]), adapted from a firm model ([[Model contract]]), or written from scratch for this deal ([[Bespoke]])? This three-way classification is not yet encoded in Wikidata — the gap between [[Model contract]] and [[Bespoke]] (a clause that *started* as a template but was modified during negotiation) has no dedicated node.
