A pre-contractual document summarising the key economic and legal terms of an envisaged transaction. In M&A, the term sheet is typically non-binding on commercial terms but binding on specific clauses such as exclusivity and confidentiality.

Its dual nature makes it ontologically interesting: the document itself is a [[Model contract]] (derived from a firm template), yet individual clauses within it span the full [[Provenance]] spectrum — from [[Boilerplate]] (e.g. governing law) to [[bespoke]] (e.g. valuation methodology, earn-out metrics).
