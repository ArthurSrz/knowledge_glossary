cWe define a Compound AI System as a system that tackles AI tasks using multiple interacting components, including multiple calls to models, retrievers, or external [[tools]]. In contrast, an AI [[Model]] is simply a [statistical model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Statistical_model), e.g., a Transformer that predicts the next [[token]] in text.
