**Control theory** is a field of control engineering and applied mathematics that deals with the control of dynamic system. The aim is to develop a [[model]] or [[algorithm]] governing the application of system [[inputs]] to drive the system to a desired state, while minimizing any _delay_, _overshoot_, or _steady-state error_ and ensuring a level of control [[stability]]; often with the aim to achieve a [[degree]] of optimality
