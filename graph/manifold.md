In mathematics, a **manifold** is a topological space that locally resembles Euclidean space **near each point**. More precisely, an n-dimensional manifold, or n-manifold, is a topological space with the [[property]] that each point has a neighborhood that is homeomorphic to an open [[subset]] of n-dimensional Euclidean space.
