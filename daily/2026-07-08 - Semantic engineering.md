Let's meet a new type of system : Semantic [[knowledge]] systems. According to Jessica Talisman, they can be built following an [[ontology]] pipeline : 

1. It starts with controlled vocabularies that ensure that terms used within an organization are referred to consistently in a given [[context]]. Those vocabularies are then attached to all [[data]] and information in a given [[environment]]
2. Then, you prepare the vocabulary to be structured as a [[taxonomy]], a hierarchy defined bu parent-child relations. 
3. The [[taxonomy]] then serves as a foundation for a thesaurus
